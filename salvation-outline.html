Salvation

Deliverance Through Christ — Discipleship Study Outline

I. What Is Salvation?

  • Definition: Deliverance from sin through Christ
  • Past: We have been saved (Justification)
  • Present: We are being saved (Sanctification)
  • Future: Complete transformation (Glorification)

II. The Human Condition

  • Created in God's image (Gen. 1:26-27)
  • The Fall brought sin & death (Gen. 3)
  • All have sinned (Rom. 3:23)
  • Spiritually dead (Eph. 2:1-3)
  • Cannot save ourselves (Eph. 2:8-9)
Romans 6:23 — "The wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus."

III. God's Redemptive Plan

  • Plan established before creation (Eph. 1:4-5)
  • "In Him" — God chose Christ as the plan; those who accept Him are chosen
  • Revealed through covenants (Abrahamic → New)
  • Fulfilled in Christ (Gal. 4:4-5)

IV. The Work of Christ

  • Incarnation: God became man (John 1:14)
  • Sinless Life: Our representative (Heb. 4:15)
  • The Cross: Substitutionary atonement (1 Pet. 2:24)
  • Resurrection: Victory over death (Rom. 4:25)
  • Intercession: He lives to intercede (Heb. 7:25)
Romans 5:8 — "While we were still sinners, Christ died for us."

V. Application of Salvation

How It Comes To Us:

  • Grace: God's unmerited favor — the source (Eph. 2:8-9)
  • Faith: Trusting in Christ — the instrument (Rom. 10:9-10)
  • Repentance: Turning from sin — the response (Acts 2:38)

What Happens:

  • Regeneration: Born again (John 3:3-8)
  • Justification: Declared righteous (Rom. 5:1, 8:1)
  • Adoption: Made children of God (Gal. 4:4-7)

VI. Fruits of Salvation

  • Sanctification: Progressive growth (Phil. 2:12-13)
  • Assurance: Nothing separates us (Rom. 8:38-39)
  • Good Works: Evidence, not cause (Eph. 2:10)
  • Perseverance: God preserves us (John 10:27-29)
Philippians 1:6 — "He who began a good work in you will carry it on to completion."

VII. The Fullness of Salvation — Now

  • Glorification: Christ glorified in us NOW (2 Cor. 3:18)
  • Resurrection: We are new creations NOW (2 Cor. 5:17)
  • Eternal Life: God dwells in us NOW (1 Cor. 6:19)
2 Corinthians 5:17 — "If anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come!"

Discussion Questions:

  1. How would you explain your need for salvation?
  2. What aspect of Christ's work means most to you?
  3. How has understanding salvation changed how you live?
  4. What questions do you have about assurance?
